About Us

This was a project inspired by the Bentonville West High School's Entomology Binder. We had created a 300 paged digital binder over about 100+ insect

Why This?

Over the time of creating this binder, me and my friends have grown a huge appreciation for bugs. As nerdy as it is, its still a very enjoyable project. As this website is made for a NWACC project, I still thought it would be a fun studying opportunity that is also educational to others outside of the competition
